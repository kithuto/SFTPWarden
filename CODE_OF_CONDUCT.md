# Code of Conduct

SFTPWarden should be a practical, respectful place to collaborate.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind, patient, and constructive.
- Assume good intent, especially when discussing bugs or operational incidents.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Share enough context for others to understand and reproduce your work.
- Redact secrets, customer data, internal hostnames, and private keys.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic disruption.
- Dismissing security concerns without review.
- Sharing credentials, private keys, tokens, or sensitive deployment details.

## Reporting

For normal project issues, use GitHub issues.

For security-sensitive behavior or vulnerability reports, follow
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) instead of opening a public issue.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or take other action
needed to keep the project safe and useful.
